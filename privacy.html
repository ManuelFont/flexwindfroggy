Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Flexwind Froggy is an educational browser game maintained by Manuel Font. This policy explains how information may be stored or collected when you use flexwindfroggy.com.

Information stored in your browser

The game uses your browser's local storage to remember game progress, answers, language, difficulty, and colorblind-mode preferences. This information stays in your browser and is not sent to the site owner. You can remove it by clearing this site's browser storage.

Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics to measure general site usage, such as page views and browser or device information. Google may use cookies, IP addresses, and similar technologies to provide these measurement services. See Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Advertising

The site uses Google AdSense to display advertising. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar identifiers to serve, measure, and personalize ads. Third parties may place and read cookies on your browser as a result of ad serving on this site. You can learn how Google uses data when you use partners' sites at Google's partner-sites page.

Your choices and consent

Where required, the site will ask for consent before using personal data for personalized advertising. You can manage advertising preferences in Google's Ad Settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the site owner through LinkedIn or Email.
